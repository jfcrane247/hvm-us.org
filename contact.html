💙 Grief Support Groups

Hope In The Valley Ministry offers faith-based grief support for those who have lost a loved one to suicide. Our groups provide a safe, compassionate space for healing.

  • ✓ Faith-based peer support groups
  • ✓ Pastoral counseling and prayer
  • ✓ Annual memorial service
  • ✓ Connection to professional counselors
