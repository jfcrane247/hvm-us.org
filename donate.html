FAQ

Donation Questions

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Hope In The Valley Ministry is organized as a non-profit charitable organization. Donations are generally tax-deductible to the extent permitted by law. Please consult your tax advisor.

How is my gift used?

Your donation funds our three core programs: suicide prevention education, grief support groups, and community outreach. You may designate your gift to a specific program area.

Can I set up a recurring gift?

Yes! Monthly and annual recurring donations provide reliable support that allows us to plan and expand our programs. Select "Monthly" or "Annual" above before donating.

Is my information secure?

Absolutely. All transactions are processed through industry-standard encrypted payment processors. We never store your financial information on our servers.
